05 — FAQ

Common questions.

The things clients usually ask before we start — straight answers, no marketing fluff.

What's a typical project timeline?
Marketing sites usually ship in about a week. Booking platforms with auth, dashboards, and scheduling are typically 1–2 weeks. We lock a timeline together after going over your needs in writing — email, WhatsApp, or whichever messenger works best for you — so you know exactly what to expect.
What's included in the price?
Design, development, deployment, and one round of structured revisions. After launch, a monthly maintenance and support plan keeps your site running — $30/month for static marketing sites, $80/month for booking platforms and database-backed systems. That covers uptime monitoring, security updates, backups, and small bugfixes, so you don't have to think about it. Hosting setup is included; you cover the actual hosting and domain fees, which stay in your name.
Do you handle hosting and domain?
By default, I host and manage everything — uptime, updates, and backups stay on my side so you don't have to think about it. For static marketing sites, I'm happy to deploy to your preferred provider and hand the keys over end to end. Booking platforms stay under my managed infrastructure — that's where the custom system lives, and keeping it on a setup I maintain is how I make sure it stays secure, fast, and reliable long-term.
How do revisions work?
Every package includes one structured revision round after design review. Small tweaks — copy edits, color or spacing nudges, micro-adjustments — are unlimited within reason. I'd rather you love the result than count rounds.
How does payment work?
Standard split is 50% upfront to start, 50% on launch. Larger booking platforms can be split into milestones. I'm based in Turkey, so international payments go through Payoneer — straightforward for clients in the US, EU, UK, and most other regions. For local clients in Turkey, standard bank transfer / IBAN works fine.
Do you offer post-launch support?
Yes — every project includes a monthly maintenance and support plan: $30/month for static marketing sites, $80/month for booking platforms and database-backed systems. It covers uptime monitoring, security updates, backups, and small bugfixes. For bigger changes, new features, or ongoing development work, we scope those separately when the time comes.
